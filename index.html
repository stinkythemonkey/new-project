Who we are


Didsbury Good Neighbours is a local charity based in the center of Didsbury park. Our charity is for those who are residents of Didsbury and the surrounding areas. We are dedicated to improve the lives of people aged 55 and over through engaging activities, social connection and community support.


Mission Statement


Our mission is to provide a difference in the older aged community to allow them to live independently, we also want to raise awareness to the local residents, businesess.

What we offer


We offer a range of social groups and activites to suit all of your abilities, needs and hobbies.

The types of activities you can expect to find here at Didsbury Good Neighbours are fitness/ exercises classes, social events, musical classes, art to computing and many more activites.

We also run events/activites that aren't on our weekly timetable as they only occur during certain times throughout the year and month.
